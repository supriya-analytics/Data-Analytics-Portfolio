# Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard

## Objective
Analyze sales, profit, quantity sold, discount impact, and profitability across regions, categories, and time.

## KPIs
- Total Sales: 68M
- Total Profit: 11M
- Total Quantity Sold: 2772
- Profit Margin: 15.59%
- Average Discount: 7.38%

## Visuals Included
- Monthly Sales vs Profit Trend
- Sales by Region
- Profit Margin by Region
- Sales & Profit by Product Category
- Product-wise Performance Table

## Key Insights
- North region generates highest sales
- Office Supplies contribute highest profit
- Profit margin remains consistent across regions
- Sales show seasonal peaks and dips

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
